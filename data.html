Data sources

We've collected some of the open data sources you might want to use to put your hack together.

Reporting crime

Officers will be on hand at the event to show and explain to you how we deal with many different types of incident using our paper-based, and partially computerised systems, including domestic violence, traffic collisions, stop and searches, premises searches, robbery, burglary, and assault.

We'd love to see just what can be achieved with mobile technologies!

police.uk data

police.uk make their raw data about crime, anti-social behaviour and outcomes available in a standard format, so that communities, local services and developers can make good use of it.

police.uk API

police.uk also make available an API to retrieve information about neighbourhood areas in all 43 English & Welsh police forces and for the Police Service of Northern Ireland.

Met area

The Metropolitan Police make their crime mapping data available as a set of tables.
