Outcomes

The hackathon resulted in 12 fascinating projects.

Projects

Each project represented a new exciting functionality that would make policing easier and more efficient. Each developer showcased what they had created at the end of the hack, and prizes were awarded in each of the categories.

Going forwards...

All those involved in the hack resolved to continue to push for change and improvement to policing - to bring about the desire for technological modernisation of the police service and so save public money and police time, making police work less arduous and disappointing, and making the public safer.
